Hospitals, Doctors and Medical services

Esquire has a tie up with an ambulance service Jeevan Ambulance

424Jeevan Ambulance 9821555528/8080608888


Gokuldham Medical Centre behind Gokuldham temple has afull variety of medical services including MD Doctors in most specialities, Dental and Eye care, X-Ray, Physiotherapy and Pathology, at very reasonable rates. The downside is that it is crowded, but it is well managed and clean.

GMC 022-41745555


Lifeline Hospital Gokuldham (near GMC) is the closest hospital for absolute emergencies. It is not hygenic, but sometimes this si the only option


Other hospitals in the vicinity are Suchak Hospital near Malad Subway, Thunga Hospital Malad West and Lifeline Malad West (SV Road). Bigger hospitals are Hiranandani (Powai), Kokilaben (Andheri West) and Lilavati (Bandra West)
